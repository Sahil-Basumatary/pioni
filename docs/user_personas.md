# User target

## 1. Student Retail Trader
- Age: 18–23
- Background: Learns finance through Reddit, YouTube, or TikTok
- Goal: Wants to understand market trends fast without reading tens articles a day
- Problem: Tools are either too technical or too vague/general
- Behaviour: Makes trades based on social hype rather than actual experience and research

## 2. Junior Investment Analyst
- Age: 21–26
- Background: New hire at a bank or fund, time-limited, under pressure
- Goal: Needs fast sentiment checks to supplement reports
- Problem: Research tools are slow or locked behind licenses
- Behaviour: Uses alternative data when available, but doesn’t have infrastructure to collect it

## 3. Curious enthusiasts
- Age: 17–30
- Background: Interested in markets, not committed to trading yet
- Goal: Learn through interaction, not youtube tutorials
- Problem: Feels excluded by complex dashboards or technical jargons
- Behaviour: Doom scrolls more than they study, but still wants to learn something real
